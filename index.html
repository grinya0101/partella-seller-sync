Private internal seller tool for Etsy operations

Manage your Etsy workflow with precision.

Partella Seller Sync is a private internal application built for streamlined Etsy seller operations. It is used to manage listings, inventory, pricing, tags, descriptions, images, and sales-related shop data through Etsy Open API.

What the application does

The application is designed as a clean internal seller tool focused on efficiency, consistency, and accurate shop data management inside a private Etsy workflow.

◇

Listing management

Supports internal workflows for listing creation, updates, pricing adjustments, tag organization, descriptions, and inventory-related maintenance.

▣

Sales data access

Reads seller-side shop sales information for internal review, structured reporting, and operational decision-making.

✦

Private automation

Used only for private seller operations. It is not a public service, not sold to users, and not intended for unrelated third-party shops.
